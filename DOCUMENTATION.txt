=====IDECIDE VOTING APP======

This serves as the user manual for idecide voting app. 

INTRODUCTION.

Idecide voting app is a blockchain based voting app designed for small institutions like schools, organizations etc that wish to conduct a free and fair election process but are faced with challenges related to transparency, trust and accountability. 

This projects mine goal is to decentralise some of the processes and make them as transparent as possible. 

1.Authentication
When a user visits the app, they are directed to a login page with a button to sign in with thier instition. As an example, I used Ndejje University. 
A user needs to be part of the instition and has an account with the university. 
On clicking, a user is redirected to a page where they are prompted to allow th process to continue
